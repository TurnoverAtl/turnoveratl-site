What’s included in every turnover

  • Kitchen and bathroom reset
  • Floors and high-touch surfaces
  • Trash removal
  • Linen reset using host-supplied inventory
  • Clear communication throughout the process

Laundry

Linens are reset using host-supplied inventory. Laundry is completed on-site when available and within the scheduled clean time. Additional loads may require additional time.

Deep cleans

Deep clean services are priced separately from standard turnovers.

If a unit requires additional time due to condition or scope beyond a standard turnover, we’ll notify you and confirm adjusted pricing before proceeding. Deep clean pricing is intentionally kept competitive.

Same-day and rush requests

Same-day or expedited turnovers are subject to availability and may incur a $25 rush fee.

Payments and deposits

Submitting a booking request does not require payment.

Payment is collected only after availability is confirmed and a cleaner is scheduled.

A $75 booking deposit is required to secure your turnover and is applied toward your total service cost. Deposits are collected via secure Square invoice.

Deposits are non-refundable within 24 hours of the scheduled start time.
