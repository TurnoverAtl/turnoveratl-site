Commercial Cleaning

We offer cleaning for select commercial spaces that value consistency, clear communication, and reasonable expectations. Our focus is on small, low-traffic offices rather than large facilities or high-volume contracts.

Request a Quote Book a Cleaning

Good fit for

  • Small professional offices
  • Therapy and counseling offices
  • Creative studios and agency spaces
  • Leasing and real estate offices
  • Office suites with shared common areas

These spaces typically need weekly or bi-weekly cleaning and have predictable layouts.

What we handle

  • Restrooms and kitchenettes
  • Floors and common surfaces
  • Trash removal
  • Break rooms and entry areas
  • Light dusting and reset

Cleaning is done during agreed time windows, often after hours or during low-traffic periods.

What we don’t currently service

  • Large office buildings
  • Warehouses or industrial spaces
  • Gyms, restaurants, or medical facilities
  • High-traffic public venues

This helps us maintain quality and reliability for the spaces we do take on.

How it works

  1. You share basic details about the space and preferred schedule.
  2. We review the request and confirm whether it’s a fit.
  3. If approved, we provide clear scope and pricing before anything is scheduled.

No long contracts. No assumptions.

Pricing

Commercial pricing depends on the size of the space, how often it’s used, and how frequently cleaning is needed. Quotes are provided after a short review.
